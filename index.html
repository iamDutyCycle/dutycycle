Duty Cycle_

IDM / Ambient / Downtempo / Instrumental

Duty Cycle (born Max L. Patyk; 1982) is a Polish musician and producer from Silesia. A pioneer of an eclectic approach to contemporary electronic music, merging tracker techniques with analog synthesis and electric guitar.

The project represents a synthesis of downtempo and atmospheric soundscapes. In the world of Duty Cycle, the principle is simple: “The soul sings, the fingers play, the machine processes.”
